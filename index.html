Opening a Demat account is the first step toward investing in the Indian stock market. You can directly begin the process by visiting the official Kotak Neo Demat Account Opening Page.

What is a Demat Account?

A Demat (Dematerialized) account stores shares and securities in electronic format. It eliminates physical share certificates and enables secure digital transactions.

Step-by-Step Process

Step 1: Visit Official Website

Go to the official Kotak Neo account opening page.

Step 2: Enter Basic Details

Provide your name, mobile number, email ID, and PAN.

Step 3: OTP Verification

Verify your mobile number using OTP.

Step 4: Aadhaar eKYC

Complete Aadhaar-based verification process.

Step 5: Upload Documents

Upload necessary documents digitally.

Step 6: Video KYC

Complete in-person verification via video.

Step 7: E-Sign

Sign the application digitally using Aadhaar OTP.
